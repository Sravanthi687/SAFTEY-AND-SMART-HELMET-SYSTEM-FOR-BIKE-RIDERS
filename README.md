# 🪖 Safety and Smart Helmet System for Bike Riders

## 📌 Introduction
The Safety and Smart Helmet System is designed to improve road safety for bike riders. 
It ensures that the rider wears a helmet, avoids drunk driving, and helps in accident detection.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- To ensure helmet usage while riding  
- To prevent drunk driving  
- To reduce road accidents  
- To provide safety alerts during accidents  

---

## 🧩 Components Used
- Helmet  
- IR Sensor (Helmet detection)  
- Alcohol Sensor  
- Vibration Sensor  
- Relay Module  
- Buzzer  
- Battery  

---

## ⚙️ Working Principle
The system works using different sensors to enhance rider safety:

- The **IR sensor** detects whether the rider is wearing a helmet.  
- If the helmet is not worn, the bike ignition will not start.  
- The **alcohol sensor** detects alcohol consumption and prevents ignition.  
- The **vibration sensor** detects accidents or sudden impacts.  
- In case of an accident, a **buzzer alert** is activated.
